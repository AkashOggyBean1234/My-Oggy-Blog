Places I Think You Must Visit!

Bouncy Hills

Last Visited in 15th February 2015

One of the only hills in Wonderland which is very much popular nowadays. These hills are the most magnificient and astonishing looking hills in Wonderland! The last time I went there in 2015, it was summer and the weather was very pleasant. One you will go there, you will never want to go away from it. It also has a camping side right besides the hills, which is a very great place for camping. The place is full of greenary and trees and it is neither crowded with trees nor any absense of them! I would really recommend going there, especially at the times of spring! In spring, a festival known as the Bouncy Hills day is celebrated in order to protect its greenary and for the people to enjoy!
